KeyWord Panda is AI (Artificial Intelligence) based free keyword generator. This keyword research tool analyzes your website, current keyword trends, Google ranking of keywords and suggest you the suitable keywords for your website. You can use these keywords for Meta Keyword, Meta Description, Taglines or Title. 

== Description ==

KeyWordPanda Analyze your website content and generate keywords for Meta Keyword, Meta Description, Tagline, Title or other related contents. Its effective algorithm can generate  different keywords for each pages. It also check for keyword density and Google Keyword trends. 

You can contact us at  support @ seorankanalyser.com  for support. 

<br />
<h3>Features</h3>
<ul>
	<li> Fast and easy to use</li>
	<li> It will grab used keywords and analyse their scope in your website </li>
	<li> It suggest you to re-write content as per valuable keywords </li>
	<li> It analyze all your content and suggest you keywords based on Data Mining technique</li>
	<li> It analyze keywords for all pages and post </li>
</ul>

<h4>How it works</h4>

This plugin just send your domain (URL) to http://seorankanalyser.com/ web site and get details data from server. All data processed in http://seorankanalyser.com and return results to your plugin's panel. 

== Installation ==

1. Download, unzip and upload to your WordPress plugins directory
2. Click on Keyword Panda from menu and get report from there
3. You can click on Keyword Panda to get report of main page and click on particular pages / post to get individual page report
